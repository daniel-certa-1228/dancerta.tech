TemperTracker

TemperTracker is LIVE at https://daniel-certa-1228.github.io/Temper-Tracker/ and please check out the repo!

TemperTracker is a tool for parents and Pediatric Behavioral Therapists to facilitate the collection, tabulation and display of behavioral data.

This app resulted from my experience as a parent of a child on the Autism Spectrum. Asked to collect data on my son's behaviors, I was handed a paper form and a pen.

I set out to create an intuitive, easy-to-use app for parents who need to track their child's challenging behavior. It displays the data in a useful way so parents can understand what's behind their child's tantrums.

TemperTracker was designed from the ground-up with mobile use in mind.
